About

Alineter is a new age fashion and lifestyle company that focuses on making you look confident and unique, so that you stand out from the crowd and be
READY FOR EVERY MOMENT.


Started by two young Fashion Enthusiasts in Bhubaneswar, Odisha with a goal to provide people with Premium Quality clothes without them having to give any stress to their wallet.


Here's what we bring to you:

With our core belief that our customers should always get the best and latest stuff, we decided to do something different than others. (Difficult choice but we knew our customers would love it and that's what our main concern is.)


  • Premium Designs So that you can walk in confidence, stand out from the crowd and make your friends even more jealous.
  • Best Price One of our major goals was to deliver premium quality products at the best price possible so that you get the best without making a dent in your wallet, Hence we did.
  • Customer Experience that you feel in love with, (hope your girlfriend won't get jealous on us)
  • Wide range of variety so that you don't fall short on options. (we hate limits just like YOU do)
  • Your very own Fashion Guide Real fashion experts are hard to find. (Especially in INDIA, where everyone claims to be an expert) But don't worry we are there for you.
    We would help you to choose the clothes that would look best on you. And we promise you won't regret it.
    There's even more; but let's keep that a secret for now. Why you ask?
    So that you can discover those added benefits after you make us a part of your lifestyle.

Customers comes First

We are really serious when it comes to our Customer's needs, desires, and concerns. Therefore what's best for you is best for us.

Every Product is made keeping you in mind so that you never miss out, stay in trend and always stand out from the crowd.

We continuously work to provide the best clothes to our customers so that they keep looking AWESOME!
