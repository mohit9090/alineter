About

ALINETER is a clothing brand started by two enthusiastic individuals which is registered under the name of Alineter Lifestyle Industry Pvt. Ltd.

This company is started with a vision of making something different in the sector of fashion and lifestyle. We are taking fashion to the next level by bringing the all moment wear.

We all love to adapt a new style every day. ALINETER is your go-to source for styles influenced by the latest fashions with an added flair. We aim to inspire our customers to be the best version of themselves and to be confident in their own skin - and of course, their outfits.
We invite you to browse our site to find just what you have been looking for. Look good, feel good, shop ALINETER today.
